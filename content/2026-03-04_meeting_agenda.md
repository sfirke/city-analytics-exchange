Our March meeting is a "coffee chat", where respondents self-select into breakout rooms based on what topic they want to discuss. Our theme is **the lifecycle stages of a project**.

Breakout rooms will be for:

- Intake process
- Ongoing work (e.g., tracking it on a Kanban board)
- Evaluate outcomes
- Telling the story

Want to participate? Join the [mailing list](/pages/join) as we'll use that to send out the meeting appointment and join link.
