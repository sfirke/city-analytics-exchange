The City Analytics Exchange community gathers virtually every two months for an hour.

## Upcoming Meetings

We meet on:
- The 2nd Wednesday of January, May, September from 1-2pm ET (10-11am PT)
- The 2nd Friday of March, July, November from 12pm-1pm ET (9am-10am PT) 

## Meeting Format

Our meetings are typically a mix of Coffee Chats (breaking out for open discussion in small groups) and Show & Tell presentations.
